How It Works

From Movement to Sound

01

CAPTURE

Camera tracking detects body position, gesture speed, and proximity in real-time

02

TRANSLATE

TouchDesigner maps movement data to audio parameters — volume, reverb depth, effect intensity, pitch modulation

03

GENERATE

Ableton Live receives OSC signals and produces sound that directly mirrors the audience's physical presence

Camera → TouchDesigner → OSC → Ableton → Speaker
